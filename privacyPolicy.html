Privacy Policy for Flag Fusion

This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected and used when you use our Android mobile application Flag Fusion. By using the App, you agree to the data practices described here. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

What Information Is Collected?

If you contact us, we my collect and process the information that you provide to us. This information inclues the content of your e-mails, comments, and messages and responses to messages.

We do not collect or store any personal data directly. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect limited data to improve functionality and performance. These services are:

  • Firebase Crashlytics
    • This tool helps us identify and fix issues within the App by collecting crash reports and technical information.
    • For more information, see the Firebase Crashlytics Privacy Policy: Crashlytics Data Processing Terms.
  • Firebase Analytics
    • This tool collects anonymized data about how the App is used to help us improve its performance and usability.
    • For more information, see the Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy: Firebase Analytics Terms.

Note: These tools are provided by Google Firebase, and the data they collect is managed in accordance with their privacy policies.

What Data Is NOT Collected?

  • No personal data such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers is collected or stored by us.
  • The App does not collect payment information, as there are no purchases or ads.
  • No user-generated content is collected or stored.

How Is Collected Data Used?

The data collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics is used to:

  • Identify and fix technical issues (e.g., crashes).
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Optimize our Application
  • Provide customer service
  • Analyse the performance of our app

This data is not used for advertising or commercial purposes.

Third-Party Services

The App may interact with third-party services, such as Google Firebase. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies to understand how your data is managed:

  • Google Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Security

We rely on Google Firebase to securely process and store data. While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

No Commercial Use

Currently, the App is not used for commercial purposes. It does not include advertisements, in-app purchases, or other monetization features.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as necessary to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here, with the "Last Updated" date clearly indicated.
